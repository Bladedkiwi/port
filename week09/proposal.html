Rexx Rental Proposal

Purpose:

  • Item

The purpose of this website is to entice users to rent Rexx Rentals' luxurious cars. This will be done by providing the user with a well-designed site that is both simple and sheek. It will also contain pages to help the user understand who Rexx Rentals is as a company, and provide lean description of each rental car.

Audience:

  • John Moola
    • Job: CEO
    • Motto: "Time is precious. Which means, excursions must be memorable."
    • Family: Wife, and older kids
    • Technology: High-end phones, tablets, and a basic work desktop
    • Time: Time is valued, things must be quick

People that rent these cars are generally wealthy, like to party, and would consider spending extra to rent a fancy car for an occasion. They have expectations of things running smoothly without a hiccup. They are all about entertaining others, and themselves. The faster they can access renting the car they want, the better. Functionality features have to be familiar, or customary to most sites.

Initial Module List:

  • Car Controller:Controls the making of the cars' content pages.
  • Car Model:Grabs all info for the car page.
  • Car View:Creates the base layout of the items in the car's info section.
  • Main:Controls all JavaScript pages
  • Utilities:Contains all versatile functions.

The idea is to have a controller dynamically create the car pages by displaying all the data for them. That way altering data for each of the car pages is easy and becomes the template. This also adds functionality for adding more cars to the list.
