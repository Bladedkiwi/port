Rexx Rental Proposal

Purpose:

Our purpose it to entice users to rent Rexx Rentals' luxurious cars, and highlight custom features on each of their cars. This will be done by providing the user with a well-designed site that is both simple and sheek. It will also contain pages to help the user understand who Rexx Rentals is as a company, and provide a description of each rental car.

Audience:

  • John Moola
    • Job: CEO
    • Motto: "Time is precious. Which means, excursions must be memorable."
    • Family: Wife, and older kids
    • Technology: High-end phones, tablets, and a basic work desktop
    • Time: Time is valued, things must be quick

People that rent these cars are generally wealthy, like to party, and would consider spending extra to rent a fancy car for an occasion. When selecting a rental car, they are all about getting the process over with. They really appreciate when the process is painless and reliable. Although they love the quick process, they also enjoy quality products.

Data:

  • JSON file: Contains all the rentable cars' info, pictures, videos. I will have to make this based on Rexx Rental's Turo Page and content provided from owner.
  • LocalStorage:
  • Possibly Google Calendar API: To see owner's rental calendar, and schedule their rental time. I'm fairly positive, this is a lot more work than I realize.

Initial Module List:

  • Car Controller: Controls the making of the cars' content pages.
  • Car Model: Grabs all info for the car page.
  • Car View: Creates the base layout of the items in the car's info.
  • Main: Controls all JavaScript pages
  • Utilities: Contains all versatile functions.

The idea is to have a controller dynamically create the car pages by displaying all the data for them. This will make adding new vehicles to the rental list easy to manage.
